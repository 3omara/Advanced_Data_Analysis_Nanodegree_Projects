Conclusions¶

Results:

  1. The most highly rated genre is documentary while being the least demanding when it comes to budget.
  2. While documentaries are a safe bet, they produce the least profit of all genres on average.
  3. Adventure is a costly movie genre while being the sixth least genre in average ratings of associated movies.
  4. Even though Adventure movies appear to be a risk, they, on average, produce a great profit (second highest in profit mean).
  5. Animation movies are the most demanding of all when it comes to budget, but they are the most rewarding in profit terms.
  6. Horror movies are low-budget, recieve lowest ratings, and produce lowest profits.
  7. Horror movies did not improve over time in terms of ratings but instead declined.
  8. Even though the budget to profit ratings of movie genres are mostly consistent, History and Western genres place higher in budget demandingness while placing relatively much lower in mean profit generation.
  9. Foreign is the most risky genre of movies as, on average, foreign genre movies have revenues lower than their budgets.

Limitations:

  1. There were a lot of data rows with missing budget and revenue values causing statistics to be less accurate depending on less data.
